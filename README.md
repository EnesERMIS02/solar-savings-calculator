# ☀️ Solar Savings Calculator

A web-based tool that helps users in Turkey estimate how much money and CO₂ they can save by switching to solar energy.

Built as part of the **Future Talent Programme – Module 201: AI for Sustainability**, supported by Citi Foundation, YGA, and UP School.

---

## 🌍 Why This Project

Turkey has one of the highest solar energy potentials in Europe, yet residential adoption remains low. This tool makes the financial case for solar energy simple and accessible for everyday users.

---

## ⚙️ How It Works

1. Select your city
2. Enter your monthly electricity bill (TL)
3. Enter your available roof size (m²)
4. Get instant estimates for:
   - Monthly energy output (kWh)
   - Monthly savings (TL)
   - Payback period (years)
   - Annual CO₂ offset (kg)
